Aurora

Explore

Polar Lights

Aurora is a province with 8 municipalities and a plethora of tourism attractions. It has its share of historical sights, such as the home of former Philippine President Manuel L. Quezon. There are many historical markers in the province, and its natural wonders include beautiful waterfalls, rich foliage on steep slopes, and panoramic beaches. Some of the activities available for tourists in Aurora include surfing, snorkeling, scuba diving, mountain climbing, and trekking.

Sabang Beach

The center of Aurora, which is located in the municipality of Baler, is the first place that every tourist visiting Aurora comes across. This place, also known as the "Paradise for surfers", is a commonly visited spot in Baler. It offers visitors the opportunity to learn how to surf with the help of local instructors. The center of Aurora is great for various activities such as sightseeing, walking, swimming, relaxing, enjoying meals and beaches.

Ermita Hill

Situated in Sitio Dicaloyongan, Barangay Zabali, Aurora, this place has a long and tragic history carved into it. The statues of people here represent the community's escape from a big tidal wave called Tromba Marina during the 18th century. This place has a staircase with an estimated 250 steps going up. Visitors can marvel at the massive artistic structures, buildings, and a wide oval at the top of the hill.

Mother Falls

Barangay Ditumabo, San Luis, Aurora is where this place is located. The huge waterfall in the middle of the forest offers a panoramic view that creates a refreshing bond with nature. To reach the waterfall, visitors can embark on a 30-minute walking adventure. Along the way, there are various fascinating things to sightsee, making the journey even more enjoyable.

Museo de Baler

This place is located in Poblacion, Baler, Aurora. It is situated near Sabang Beach, and there is a wide green park nearby where visitors can relax and chat with friends. The place showcases the history of Baler and serves as a cultural hub. Visitors can find artifacts, paintings, memorabilia, and ethnic exhibits of the province. A monument statue of former President Manuel Quezon can be seen outside the place. Visitors can also find his belongings, other artifacts, and commemorative paintings from the Spanish colonial period inside the place.

Discalarin Cove

This place is located in Barangay Zabali, Baler, Aurora. It is a 10-minute drive to Aurora's piece of paradise, which is famous for its white sand and crystal blue beach. Visitors can also find the Lighthouse on top of the hill, which offers stunning views that cannot be found anywhere else. This place is perfect for those who want to free their mind from all worries. Visitors can either choose to rest beside the sea or discover the wide areas of Baler from the top of the lighthouse.
