The Philippines.

The Philippines is a Southeast Asian country with rich cultural heritage and stunning natural beauty, including over 7,000 islands with world-famous beaches, rice terraces, and chocolate hills. Its unique melting pot of cultures and warm hospitality from friendly locals make it an unforgettable destination to explore and immerse oneself in vibrant culture. Visitors can create lasting memories by experiencing the breathtaking beauty of the Philippines.

Taytay, Rizal

Taytay, a first-class municipality in the Philippines' Rizal province, is known as the Garments and Woodworks Capital, with a land area of 38.8 square kilometers and about 400 thousand residents.

Taguig, Metro Manila

Taguig is a city in the Philippines with a rich history and a modern outlook. It was named after its original farmer-fishermen inhabitants and still retains its provincial charm. Taguig is also known as the "Probinsyudad" - a rising star in Metro Manila.

Learn More

Aurora, Isabela

Aurora is a Philippine province with 8 municipalities and a wealth of tourist attractions, including historical sights, natural wonders, and outdoor activities like surfing and mountain climbing. It is also the birthplace of former Philippine President Manuel L. Quezon.
